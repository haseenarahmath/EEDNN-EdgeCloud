Welcome to the project Early Exit DNN Analysis!!!

  • Among various machine learning algorithms, deep learning has proved its remarkable capabilities in data processing and analysis.
  • With more complex network layers, DNN can extract more features, but at the cost of increased latency and energy consumption during the inference process.
  • For a real-time application with energy and resource-constrained devices, inferring result from a complex and large DNN is really a challenging task.
  • Creating branches and introducing early exit points can reduce inference time and thereby energy consumption while meeting the desired accuracy.
  • The Objective of the project is to analyze the behavior of branchy deep learning neural networks with respect to exit points, threshold, and branching.
